Biography

I was born on May 14th and have lived in Charleston, SC my entire life. I attended Buist Academy for Advanced studies for my elementary and middle school years. I later attended James Island Charter Highschool and graduated in May 2020. In august I would then attend the University of South Carolina-Columbia and have my Freshman year there. I later transferred to the College of Charleston and am currently finishing my remaining years of college. I expect to graduate in May 2024.

Hometown

Charleston South Carolina is the oldest and largest city in South Carolina as well as being one of the most popular. Founded in 1670, Charleston remains as a historical flashback to the past with it's preserved historic Architecture, roads and sites. Besides the already historic and preserved architecture, charleston houses numerous natural sites and the area has many native and non-native flora that enhance the overall environment and makes Charleston both a historic as well as natural coastal site.

Why I started photography

I've always been interested in nature and the environment ever since I was a young boy. Collecting rocks, watching natural geographic, reading numerous books of the natural world. For the longest time I've felt very enraptured in it. Furthermore two photographers in my life that are the parents of a close friend of mine also sowed the seeds to one of my favorite hobbies. However I started to really enjoy photography exactly in 2022, using a loaned camera by a friend. I went around the peninsula taking pictures of flora I felt they would like in an effort to impress them. However as I traveled around the peninsula, I later found to really enjoy the habit of walking around, sightseeing and taking pictures of things I found pleasing to the eye even after me and this friend eventually drifted aprart. I would later recieve my own camera from my mother as a christmas gift and ever since then I've gone around and taken my own photos typically centered around flowers, nature, animals and architecture.
